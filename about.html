About Nina Hotels

A premier hospitality destination in the heart of Kubwa, Abuja

Who We Are

A Legacy of
Hospitality

Nina Hotels is a popular hospitality and relaxation venue located at Plot 3 Romanus Nzekwe Street, Phase 4, Kubwa, Abuja. Known for its extensive sports and recreational facilities, it functions as both a luxury hotel and an active social center for families, groups and business travelers.

We are committed to delivering a world-class experience — combining modern comforts with warm, attentive service and a vibrant atmosphere that makes every guest feel truly welcome.

Sports & Recreation

Outdoor courts for basketball, lawn tennis, table tennis, badminton, volleyball, football and an open gym.

Dining & Nightlife

On-site restaurant with local and international dishes, plus an open-air bar and vibrant weekend events.

Standard Services

Free Wi-Fi, complimentary breakfast, free parking, room service, air conditioning and flat-screen TVs.

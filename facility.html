About Our Property

Nina Hotels,
Kubwa Abuja

Nina Hotels, located at Plot 3 Romanus Nzekwe Street in Kubwa, Abuja, offers a modern and welcoming accommodation experience. Situated in the bustling Kubwa district, the hotel provides easy access to key parts of the city.

Guests can expect spacious rooms, air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs, and an array of in-room facilities to enhance their stay. Whether traveling for work or relaxation, Nina Hotels ensures a restful and enjoyable experience.

The hotel is known to be one of the best in Abuja, offering exceptional recreational experiences including volleyball courts, fitness centre, outdoor pool, tennis courts, business centre and restaurants.
